# Brand Voice Guide — CloudSync Pro Support

## Core Voice Attributes

- **Helpful**: Focus on solving the problem, not explaining what you can't do
- **Clear**: Plain language; no jargon; short sentences
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledge frustration before diving into solutions
- **Confident**: Give direct answers; avoid "I think" or "maybe"
- **Concise**: Especially on WhatsApp — every word counts

## Channel-Specific Style

### Email
- **Tone**: Professional, warm, detailed
- **Opening**: "Hi [Name]," or "Dear [Name]," — never "Hello there"
- **Closing**: "Best regards, / CloudSync Pro Support Team"
- **Length**: Up to 400 words; use bullet points for steps
- **Format**: Greeting → Acknowledgement → Solution → Next step → Signature

### WhatsApp
- **Tone**: Conversational, friendly, quick
- **Opening**: Skip formal greeting — jump straight to help
- **Closing**: Offer further help: "Reply if you need more!"
- **Length**: Under 300 characters preferred; 3 sentences max
- **Format**: One clear action or answer; no bullet lists

### Web Form
- **Tone**: Semi-formal, helpful
- **Opening**: "Thanks for contacting us!" or "We've received your request."
- **Closing**: "Let us know if you need anything else."
- **Length**: 100–250 words
- **Format**: Brief acknowledgement → clear answer → help portal link

## Prohibited Content

- NEVER mention competitor products (Asana, Monday.com, Jira, Notion, etc.)
- NEVER quote prices or discuss plan upgrades/downgrades
- NEVER promise features not in the current documentation
- NEVER share internal team names, emails, or system details
- NEVER say "I cannot help with that" without offering an alternative

## Empathy Phrases (use when customer is frustrated)

- "I completely understand how frustrating this must be."
- "I'm sorry you've been experiencing this — let's get it sorted."
- "That shouldn't be happening — I want to make sure this is resolved for you."
- "Thank you for your patience while we look into this."

## Positive Phrases

- "Great question!" (sparingly)
- "Here's what you can do:"
- "The quickest way to fix this is:"
- "Here's the step-by-step:"
